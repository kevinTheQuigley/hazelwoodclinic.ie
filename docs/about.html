About Hazelwood Clinic

Hazelwood Clinic is a frontline, private clinic offering a comprehensive range of psychological services for adults, children, and adolescents in a warm, non-judgmental environment.

Our team of experienced Senior Clinical Psychologists brings over a decade of experience across diverse settings, including Community-Based Teams, Disability Services, and both Child and Adolescent and Adult Mental Health Services.

We believe in providing evidence-based, person-centered care that acknowledges and respects the unique experiences, needs, and goals of each client.

Services Available

Hazelwood Clinic offers a wide range of services related to the neurodiversity-affirming identification and support of various forms of neurodivergence. We also assess and treat forms of learning, emotional, behavioral, mental health, and other psychological difficulties.

In addition to offering individual therapy, consultation and assessment services, we also provide specialized support for:

  • Anxiety and mood disorders
  • Trauma and PTSD
  • Neurodiversity-related issues (ADHD, Autism)
  • LGBTQI+ and polyamorous community support
  • Identity and relational challenges
  • Treatment-resistant mental health disorders

At Hazelwood Clinic, our clinicians work with clients of all ages to develop developmentally appropriate evidence-based plans to help individuals work to their potentials in all settings, while addressing any barriers that might be standing in the way of navigating life's challenges.

The clinic also liaises with external professionals and refers clients — where necessary — for other specialist services (e.g., Speech and Language Therapy, Occupational Therapy, Psychiatry, etc.).

Our Therapeutic Approach

We integrate multiple evidence-based therapeutic modalities tailored to each client's unique needs:

Compassionate Care

We create a safe, supportive space where clients feel respected and understood, establishing trust through empathetic listening and genuine concern for your wellbeing.

Evidence-Based Practice

Our interventions are grounded in scientific research and validated therapeutic methods, ensuring you receive the most effective care for your specific concerns.

Holistic Perspective

We consider all aspects of your life—mind, body, relationships, and environment—recognizing that wellbeing encompasses more than symptom reduction.

Collaborative Partnership

We work together with you as active participants in the therapeutic process, establishing goals together and adapting our approach based on your feedback and progress.

Our Professional Standards

All clinicians at Hazelwood Clinic maintain the highest professional standards and credentials:

  • Registered with the Psychological Society of Ireland (PSI)
  • Doctoral-level qualification in clinical psychology
  • Regular continuing professional development training
  • Specialized training in evidence-based therapeutic approaches including CBT, EMDR, IFS, ACT, CFT, and Psychodynamic Psychotherapy
  • Extensive experience in both public and private healthcare settings
  • Commitment to ethical practice and confidentiality

We are dedicated to providing high-quality care and continually enhancing our knowledge and skills to better serve our clients.
