Stephen offers comprehensive workshops and training programmes for organisations, educational institutions, and community groups, sharing expertise in psychology and evidence-based practices.

Professional Training & Development

Stephen brings extensive experience in delivering high-quality workshops and training sessions tailored to the specific needs of different audiences. His approach combines academic rigor with practical application, ensuring participants gain both theoretical understanding and actionable skills.

Mental Health & Wellbeing Workshops

Comprehensive training on psychological principles and mental health awareness:

  • Understanding trauma and its impact across the lifespan
  • Recognising and responding to mental health challenges
  • Building resilience and coping strategies
  • Creating psychologically safe environments
  • Supporting individuals with neurodevelopmental differences

Educational Institution Training

Specialised workshops for schools, colleges, and universities:

  • Supporting students with anxiety and emotional difficulties
  • Understanding ADHD and autism in educational settings
  • Implementing trauma-informed practices in schools
  • Staff wellbeing and burnout prevention
  • Creating inclusive learning environments

Parenting & Family Support

Evidence-based guidance for parents and caregivers:

  • Understanding child development and behaviour
  • Supporting children's emotional regulation
  • Navigating challenging behaviours with compassion
  • Building strong parent-child relationships
  • Supporting neurodivergent children and families

Healthcare & Social Services Training

Professional development for healthcare and social service providers:

  • Trauma-informed care principles and implementation
  • Working with vulnerable populations
  • Secondary trauma prevention and staff support
  • Evidence-based therapeutic approaches
  • Ethical considerations in complex cases

Community & Client Group Education

Accessible workshops designed for community members and client groups:

  • Understanding mental health and reducing stigma
  • Building emotional resilience and coping skills
  • Stress management and self-care strategies
  • Mindfulness and wellbeing practices
  • Peer support and community connection

Experience & Credentials

Stephen has delivered workshops and training for a range of organisations including the HSE, Tusla, and several Irish universities. He has guest lectured on doctoral psychology programmes at the University of Limerick, University of Galway, and Mary Immaculate College, and has provided training for secondary schools, parenting groups, and client communities.
