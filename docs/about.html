About Me

Services Available

I offer a comprehensive range of psychological services related to the neurodiversity-affirming identification and support of various forms of neurodivergence. I also assess and treat forms of learning, emotional, behavioral, mental health, and other psychological difficulties.

In addition to offering individual therapy, consultation and assessment services, I also provide specialized support for:

  • Anxiety and mood disorders
  • Trauma and PTSD
  • Neurodiversity-related issues (ADHD, Autism)
  • LGBTQI+ and polyamorous community support
  • Identity and relational challenges
  • Treatment-resistant mental health disorders

I work with clients of all ages to develop developmentally appropriate evidence-based plans to help individuals work to their potentials in all settings, while addressing any barriers that might be standing in the way of navigating life's challenges.

I also liaise with external professionals and refer clients — where necessary — for other specialist services (e.g., Speech and Language Therapy, Occupational Therapy, Psychiatry, etc.).

About Stephen

I am a registered clinical psychologist with over a decade of experience working with adults and children in both public and private settings. My professional background includes extensive work across diverse settings including Community-Based Teams, Disability Services, and both Child and Adolescent and Adult Mental Health Services.

My therapeutic approach is influenced by evidence-based modalities including Internal Family Systems (IFS), Compassion Focused Therapy, and Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT). I believe in creating a safe, supportive environment where clients feel respected and understood, establishing trust through empathetic listening and genuine concern for your wellbeing.

I am committed to providing person-centered care that acknowledges and respects the unique experiences, needs, and goals of each client, working collaboratively to develop effective therapeutic strategies.

My Therapeutic Approach

I integrate multiple evidence-based therapeutic modalities tailored to each client's unique needs:

Compassionate Care

I create a safe, supportive space where clients feel respected and understood, establishing trust through empathetic listening and genuine concern for your wellbeing.

Evidence-Based Practice

My interventions are grounded in scientific research and validated therapeutic methods, ensuring you receive the most effective care for your specific concerns.

Holistic Perspective

I consider all aspects of your life—mind, body, relationships, and environment—recognizing that wellbeing encompasses more than symptom reduction.

Collaborative Partnership

We work together as active participants in the therapeutic process, establishing goals together and adapting my approach based on your feedback and progress.
