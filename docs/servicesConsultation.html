Consultation Services

Dr. Quigley welcomes collaboration with other mental health professionals and is available to provide expert consultation, second opinions, or adjunctive support alongside existing treatment. Our consultation services include:

Clinical Case Consultation

Expert guidance for complex or treatment-resistant cases:

  • Diagnostic clarification and treatment planning
  • Case conceptualization from multiple theoretical perspectives
  • Guidance on addressing therapeutic impasses
  • Recommendations for additional assessment or intervention approaches
  • Support for cases involving trauma, neurodiversity, or complex presentations

Supervision for Mental Health Professionals

Supportive supervision for early-career psychologists and therapists:

  • Clinical skills development and enhancement
  • Ethical decision-making guidance
  • Support for personal and professional development
  • Case-based learning and reflective practice
  • Preparation for professional registration and accreditation

Collaborative Care Coordination

Coordination with other healthcare providers for integrated care:

  • Multidisciplinary team consultation
  • Liaison with physicians, psychiatrists, and other specialists
  • Input for comprehensive treatment planning
  • Supporting transitions between different levels of care
  • Coordinated approach for complex or high-risk cases

Therapeutic Approach Integration

Guidance on integrating evidence-based therapeutic approaches:

  • Training in specific therapeutic modalities (CBT, EMDR, IFS, etc.)
  • Support for integrating multiple approaches
  • Adapting therapeutic techniques for specific populations
  • Implementing evidence-based protocols
  • Enhancing therapeutic effectiveness through tailored approaches

Neurodiversity-Affirming Practices

Specialized consultation on working with neurodivergent individuals:

  • Understanding neurodiversity from a strengths-based perspective
  • Adapting clinical practices for neurodivergent clients
  • Creating neurodiversity-affirming environments
  • Addressing sensory and executive functioning needs
  • Supporting neurodivergent individuals across different contexts

Benefits of Professional Consultation

  • Enhanced clinical effectiveness and client outcomes
  • Fresh perspectives on challenging cases
  • Reduced professional isolation and burnout
  • Opportunities for professional development
  • Support for ethical decision-making
  • Access to specialized knowledge and expertise
  • Increased confidence in clinical approach
