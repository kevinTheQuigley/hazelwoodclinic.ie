Resources

Stephen has compiled a collection of trusted resources to support your mental health journey. These tools complement the therapeutic work we do at Hazelwood Clinic, but are not substitutes for professional help if you're experiencing distress.

Crisis Resources

If you or someone you know is experiencing a mental health emergency, please use these resources:

  • Emergency Services: 999/112
  • Samaritans Ireland: 116 123 (24/7 support)
  • Pieta House: 1800 247 247 (suicide prevention helpline)
  • Text About It: Text HELLO to 50808 (24/7 crisis text service)
  • Your GP or nearest Emergency Department

Recommended Books

These books offer valuable insights and practical strategies for various mental health concerns.

The Compassionate Mind

By Paul Gilbert

Based on Compassion-Focused Therapy, this book offers techniques to develop self-compassion and overcome self-criticism.

The Body Keeps the Score

By Bessel van der Kolk

Explores the effects of trauma on the body and mind, and offers pathways to recovery and healing.

Unwinding Anxiety

By Judson Brewer

Science-based approach to breaking the cycle of worry and fear to heal the anxious mind.

No Self, No Problem

By Chris Niebauer

How neuropsychology is helping us understand mindfulness and the benefits of present-moment awareness.
