Duration 1-2 sessions

Child and Adolescent Psychoeducational Assessment

This in-depth assessment explores your child's cognitive and academic profile to better understand how they learn. It typically includes:

  • Cognitive (IQ) testing
  • Academic attainment testing
  • Review of school reports
  • Interview with a teacher or school staff member

This comprehensive evaluation can help identify specific learning difficulties and guide appropriate educational supports or interventions. A school based observation can also be conducted if required.

Duration 4-5 sessions
Includes Comprehensive report & recommendations

Court-Mandated Assessment

Specialized psychological evaluations for legal proceedings such as Post Traumatic Stress Disorder with detailed documentation.

Sessions Multiple appointments as needed
Includes Comprehensive legal report
