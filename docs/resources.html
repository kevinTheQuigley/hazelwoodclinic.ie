Resources

Stephen has compiled a collection of trusted resources to support your mental health journey. These tools complement the therapeutic work we do together, but are not substitutes for professional help if you're experiencing distress.

Crisis Resources

If you or someone you know is experiencing a mental health emergency, please use these resources:

  • Emergency Services: 999/112
  • Samaritans Ireland: 116 123 (24/7 support)
  • Pieta House: 1800 247 247 (suicide prevention helpline)
  • Text About It: Text HELLO to 50808 (24/7 crisis text service)
  • Your GP or nearest Emergency Department

Need Personalised Support?

While these resources can be helpful, they are not substitutes for professional support. If you're struggling with mental health concerns, we're here to help.
