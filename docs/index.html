Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services.

What types of therapy do you offer?

At Hazelwood Clinic, we offer a variety of evidence-based therapeutic approaches, including Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT), Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing (EMDR), Internal Family Systems (IFS), and more. Our clinicians tailor their approach to meet your specific needs.

How long does therapy typically last?

The duration of therapy varies based on individual needs and goals. While some clients may experience significant improvements in 8-12 sessions, others may benefit from longer-term support. We regularly review progress and adjust our approach accordingly to ensure you're receiving the right level of care.

Do you offer both in-person and online sessions?

Yes, we provide both in-person appointments at our clinic and secure online sessions. Many of our services can be delivered effectively through telehealth, offering flexibility and accessibility for those who prefer or require remote options.

What age groups do you work with?

Our services are available to children, adolescents, and adults. We have specialists experienced in working with different age groups and developmental stages, ensuring appropriate and effective care for all our clients.
